PROJECT 02

Car Sales Performance Dashboard

A two-page Power BI dashboard analyzing car sales performance through YTD and MTD metrics, regional trends, and detailed dealer-level insights across multiple car brands.

KEY INSIGHTS
→SUVs dominated at 26.9% of total sales, with Hatchbacks close behind at 22.3%
→Pale White (47%) was the most purchased color, followed by Black and Red
→Top performing regions were Austin, Scottsdale, Aurora, Greenville, and Middletown
→Chevrolet led brand performance at $27.11M, followed by Ford at $25.43M and Dodge at $25.02M
→YTD Total Sales reached $371.2M with 13.3K cars sold and a consistent upward weekly trend
